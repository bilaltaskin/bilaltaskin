Welcome!

This page aims to provide students with additional materials and visual representations to complement their learning experience throughout the Mathematics for Economists course. Therefore, while it will enhance your understanding and provide valuable insights, you should not consider it exhaustive. Instead, it is highly recommended that you rely on the materials and guidance offered on eCampus portal as your primary learning resources.

Please be advised that certain derivations and proofs may present considerable challenges and extend beyond the confines of this course. Should you choose to pursue them, you are welcome to do so at your discretion.

On the other hand, you will also find valuable resources, such as problem sets, solutions, and lecture notes related to further advanced modules in the program.

Some well-known book references are listed below while you can navigate through the index menu for the materials.

Good luck!
